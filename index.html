Blue Origin

suborbital flight

New Shepard suborbital rocket from Blue Origin on July 20, 2021 for the first time in history company launched a capsule with people, with the richest man in the world Jeff Bezos on board.

New Shepard, 18 m high and 4 m wide, is a reusable spacecraft. Booster the installation raises a pressurized passenger ship to a height of about 76 km. Pulse carries the capsule up into space.

After that, she descends to the ground under three parachutes. booster too returns home.
